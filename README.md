# cancer-patient-analysis-and-prediction

1. Introduction

Cancer is one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide, and early detection plays a crucial role in improving patient survival rates. 
With the advancement of data science and machine learning,
predictive models can assist healthcare professionals in identifying cancer at an early stage based on patient data.

2. Problem Statement

Diagnosing cancer accurately and at an early stage remains a significant challenge due to the complexity of medical
data and variations in symptoms among patients. Traditional diagnostic methods can be time-consuming and may not always 
provide quick insights.
This project aims to develop a machine learning-based system that can predict
the likelihood of cancer in patients using historical medical data.

3. Objectives
   
To build a predictive model that can classify whether a patient has cancer or not.
To analyze patient data and identify key factors influencing cancer diagnosis.
To improve early detection and support medical decision-making.
To evaluate the performance of different machine learning algorithms.
